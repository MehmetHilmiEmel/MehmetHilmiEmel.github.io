Education Life

I started my education journey in 2006-2007 in Kartal/Istanbul. Medine Tayfur Sökmen primary school was my first step on the journey.

My second step on my education journey was Yunus Emre Middle School in Ümraniye/İstanbul. Since my family moved to Ümraniye from Kartal, I had to go to a middle school in Ümraniye.

The third step on my education journey was ECA Elginkan Anatolian High School. I was lucky to have an education in this high school. Because I have met great Teachers who have played a significant role in developing my mindset and mentality.

Moreover, my teachers in HighSchool taught me some important properties such as having self-motivation to achieve, investigating, searching, and trying to find correct resources for learning

And the final step on my education journey is BAU (Bahcesehir University). Firstly I started the Civil Engineering department at Bau. Then I didn't like my department and I did an undergraduate transfer. I have switched my department from Civil Engineering to Electric-Electronic Engineering. In Junior year I decided to double major in Computer Engineering.
